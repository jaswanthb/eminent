Project Introduction

Eminent Estates venture is spread across 30 acres land, located in Shankarpally, Hyderabad. It comprises around 100 farm plots. Each farm plot contains a stylish farm house of 1200 sq.yds, a lawn area of 3500 sq.fts and a variety of plants including 120 landscaping plants. Additional to this 12 types of seasonal fruit plantations are also provided. All the plants are under supervision of a dedicated in-house farm maintenance team. The Farm land owns a well-developed landscaped gardens with modern facilities such as swimming pool, a tennis court, kids play area and a car garage. The farm land has been gifted with rich resources such as abundant water, naturally nutrient rich soil, suitable climatic conditions, easy access farming at peaceful and pollution free environment.
